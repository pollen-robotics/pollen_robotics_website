## Open from the first sketch

At Pollen Robotics we believe robots should be **understandable, hackable and
yours**. That is why every product we ship comes with its hardware designs and
its software in the open.

## What that means in practice

- The full source of the on-robot stack is public.
- Community apps run on the same store our own apps use.
- You can fork, extend and redistribute.

Stay tuned - this blog will follow the journey of our robots, from Reachy Mini
to whatever hatches next.
