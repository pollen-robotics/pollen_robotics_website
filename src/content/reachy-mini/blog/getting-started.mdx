## The app store, in a nutshell

Every Reachy Mini app is a Hugging Face Space tagged for the robot. The
[apps page](/reachy-mini) lists the JS apps that have been categorized and
moderated, ready to open in one click.

## Shipping your own

1. Create a Space and tag it so it shows up in the catalog.
2. Add a short description and an icon under `public/icon.svg`.
3. Publish - it appears in the store after the next refresh.

That is it. No gatekeeping, no manual submission queue: the catalog is built
directly from the community's Spaces.
